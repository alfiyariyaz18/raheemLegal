Needed A TDRA Approval For E-Commerce?

TRA as TDRA

The UAE telecoms has changed its name from the Telecommunications Regulatory Authority (TRA) to the Telecommunications and Digital Government Regulatory Authority (TDRA).

What is TDRA approval in E- Commerce?

Individuals, Businesses and establishments that practice online activity like e-Commerce, e-Businesses that provide products and services online can apply for NOC (NO Objection Certificate) for practicing. In the NOC request, can either attach the App link or attach an active website link and mention that the App will be available on the website after it becomes available in the App store.

No objection letter to declare that TDRA has no concerns about conducting an e-activity on websites and social media accounts in the UAE. If the applicant has more than one channel including social media, mobile apps, and websites to provide the service, must submit a separate request for each channel.
